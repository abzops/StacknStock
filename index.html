SNS-SEC-02 / Product

What is
Stack n Stock?

Stack n Stock is a modular warehouse infrastructure platform built on ISO standard 40-foot container frames. Each pod arrives as a live, operational system — pre-wired for power, pre-configured for AS/RS, ready to interconnect.

Unlike traditional warehousing, pods are portable assets. They can be relocated between facilities, sold, or restructured without construction permits or capital write-downs.

Factory-Built & Pre-TestedPods leave our facility fully commissioned — racking, MHE, electrical, control systems installed and verified.
ISO 668 Compliant FrameUniversal corner-casting geometry. Travels on standard flatbeds, cranes, and ship frames worldwide.
WMS & ERP ReadyOpen API connectivity for integration with any warehouse management or ERP platform on day one.

SNS-SEC-03 / Process

How it
works

STEP · 01

Site Assessment

Our engineers survey your facility, map material flow, and identify pod placement zones and utility hookup points.

STEP · 02

Pod Configuration

Select module type — storage, picking, or AS/RS — and specify SKU count, throughput targets, and MHE requirements.

STEP · 03

Factory Build

Pods are constructed at our manufacturing facility, pre-fitted with racking, conveyors, electrical, and control systems.

STEP · 04

Deploy & Connect

Pods are delivered and craned into position. Connect utilities. System is live within 72 hours of arrival on site.

STEP · 05

Scale On Demand

Order more pods as demand grows. Redeploy to new locations. Stack vertically. Realign your entire operation.
